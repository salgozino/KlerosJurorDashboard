General Information

This is the Dashboard of the Kleros Court. Here you can find metrics about the amount of disputes, jurors, stakes, etc. In addition, in the Juror Odds section, you can estimate your chances of being drawn in the different courts.

The source code can be found on github. If you found issues or want some improvement, please open an issue there.

Kleros is a decentralized dispute platform. For more information, visit kleros.io
